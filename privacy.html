Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 9, 2025
Last Updated: August 9, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to RizzTipz! We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Sign-In Data: When you choose to sign in with Google or Apple, we receive basic account information such as your email address and account identifier to create and maintain your account
  • Authentication: We use Google and Apple's secure authentication systems to verify your identity

App Usage Data

  • Tip Viewing Status: We track which daily tips you have viewed to maintain your progress and streak
  • Quiz Responses: We collect and store your answers to our "Rizz" quizzes to calculate and display your quiz scores
  • Streak Counter: We track your daily app usage to maintain your streak counter (number of consecutive days you've used the app)

Information We Don't Collect

  • We do not collect additional personal information beyond what's provided through Google/Apple sign-in
  • We do not access your device's camera, microphone, contacts, or photos (beyond sign-in permissions)
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect information about other apps on your device
  • We do not store payment or credit card information (handled entirely by Apple)

How We Use Your Information

  • Account Management: We use your Google/Apple sign-in information to create and maintain your account, and to sync your data across devices
  • Progress Tracking: We store which tips you've viewed and your quiz responses to maintain your progress, streaks, and quiz scores
  • App Functionality: Your quiz answers are used to calculate your "Rizz" scores and provide personalized results
  • Payment Processing: When you make an optional tip, payment information is processed securely by Apple's payment system - we do not handle or store your payment details
  • App Improvement: We may use aggregated, non-personal usage data to understand how the app is being used and to make improvements

Data Storage and Security

  • Your account information, tip viewing progress, and quiz responses are stored securely using Google Firebase, a cloud-based platform with industry-standard security measures
  • We use Firebase Authentication for secure sign-in and Firebase Firestore for data storage
  • Your data may be synced across your devices when you sign in with the same Google/Apple account
  • Firebase employs encryption, access controls, and other security measures to protect your information
  • Payment transactions are handled entirely by Apple's secure payment infrastructure
  • If you delete your account or uninstall the app, you can request deletion of your data by contacting us

Third-Party Services

Google Firebase: We use Google Firebase for authentication and secure data storage. Firebase's Privacy Policy governs how Google handles this infrastructure.

Apple App Store: We use Apple's payment processing system for optional tips. Apple's privacy policy governs how they handle payment information.

Google Sign-In: When you sign in with Google, Google's Privacy Policy governs the authentication process. We only receive basic account information needed for app functionality.

Apple Sign-In: When you sign in with Apple, Apple's Privacy Policy governs the authentication process. We only receive the information you choose to share.

Children's Privacy

RizzTipz is designed for youth and we take children's privacy seriously. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • We do not require users to provide any personal information to use the app
  • Parents can contact us with any privacy concerns regarding their child's use of the app

Your Rights and Choices

  • Data Access: You can view your quiz scores, tip viewing progress, and streak information within the app
  • Data Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at rizztipz@gmail.com
  • Account Deletion: You can stop using the sign-in features and request account deletion at any time
  • Opt-Out: All features are optional - you can use the app without signing in, though some features like cross-device syncing won't be available

No Ads Policy

RizzTipz is completely free and ad-free. We do not work with advertising networks or collect data for advertising purposes.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify users of significant changes through the app or app store updates
  • Continue to protect your privacy in accordance with this policy

Legal Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for EU users
  • Apple App Store privacy requirements
  • Google Firebase and Google Cloud Platform terms of service

Summary

RizzTipz collects minimal data to provide app functionality. We store your Google/Apple sign-in information, track which tips you've viewed, and save your quiz responses to calculate scores and maintain streaks. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple's secure system. We don't share your data with third parties or use it for advertising.
